Citing Nilearn¶

This package can be lightweight and efficient because it relies on great toolboxes (Nilearn, Scikit Learn). A huge amount of work goes into nilearn. In addition, the Parietal team needs the citations to the paper in order to justify paying a software engineer on the project. To guarantee the future of the toolkit, if you use it, please cite this paper .
